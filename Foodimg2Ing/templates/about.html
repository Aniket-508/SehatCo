SehatCo : Smart Food Recommendation System

ABSTRACT :

According to the scientists, COVID-19 is here to stay as they expect the virus that causes COVID-19 to become endemic.After the second wave, the daily average of COVID-19 cases in India is spiralling down, even though there still are isolated outbreaks and a rising positivity rate recorded in some states. However, despite this, there's some positive silver lining, which is the growing pace of vaccination in the country. While we may not be able to bid goodbye to COVID-19 as yet, it might be the time the viral outbreak may have reached a state of 'endemicity' in the country, given the rather low levels of transmission right now and the already largely exposed population in the country. But, with the virus very much an active threat, and possibilities of a potentially threatening third wave is looking like an imminent threat. In such a scenario food or the eating habits of the people need to improve with the passage of time.Our application will be able to tell the user whether the food he is eating will help him in increasing his immunity against the coronavirus. Then, on the basis of the output from our system the user will come to know which ingredients he has to look for in his recipe to achieve his goal of strengthening his immune system against the virus.
